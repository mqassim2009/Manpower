Track employee attendance, working hours, and leave management with our advanced tracking system.

**Features:**

Evaluate and manage employee performance with structured review processes and goal tracking.

Streamline payroll processing with automated calculations and compliance management.

Generate comprehensive reports and gain insights into your workforce with powerful analytics tools.

**Features:**
